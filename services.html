Our Services

Restaurant and Bar

In our restaurant you can enjoy a fresh cooked meal. We have a wide range of dishes to chose from and a widely renowned chef, who can prepare you anything you want on the spot. 
At night, you can relax over glass of wine or anything you want at our bar.
Plus, you can book our restaurant services for your wedding, anniversary or business lunch.

Jacuzzi and Sauna

Either if you are on a vacation, or a business trip, one of the most important aspects of a healthy life is your well-being.
With that in our mind, we have provided you the perfect solution of relaxation after a long day of sightseeing or working: jacuzzi and sauna. 
By accessing these services, you will have the opportunity to forget about all of your daily problems and unwind.  

Conference Room

One precious addition to our services is the conference room.
If your company, team or organization needs a place for a conference/workshop, we are your best choice!
The conference room has a capacity of 120 people, a whiteboard,a video-projector, a microphone. Also,during breaks we offer coffee and bakeries/snacks, which are included in the payment of the conference room.

Fitness Room


We know how important is exercise for you, so our offer includes a fitness room, open 24/7. 
This way, you will be able to maintain your routine while you're away from home, and keep doing what you feel is best for both your physical and mental health. 

Prices may differ depending on what service you want and your preferences.
For Sauna, Jacuzzi and Fitness room we have fixed prices: 10€/day. 
For more information regarding the restaurant and conference room contact us.
