Accommodation  

Single Room

Single Rooms

All of our single rooms are styled with elegance and a touch of minimalism, making them the perfect mix of aesthetics and practicality.

These rooms are perfect for someone travelling alone or a weekend getaway. 
 
The rooms are fully equipped with WI-FI, Coffee machine, hair-drier, iron, plus iron table, and paid-access to our following services: spa, fitness room, restaurant and bar. 

Double Room

Double Rooms

These rooms are ideal for you, if you want extra space and more comfort.
What this type of room has to offer in addition to the single room is: parking place, a kitchenette and a bathroom with bathtub.    

Flat

Flat

If you want the ultimate luxurious experience, you should choose one of our flats for your stay. 

Our flats include a living room, a bedroom, bathroom with hydro-massage bathtub and a kitchen.

For this option, you can enjoy all of our services for free and make the most of your stay.
