About us



Two words which we had in mind when building and creating this hotel were HOME and FAMILY.

Our main goal is for our clients to feel at home, when they come and stay in our hotel.

Each member of the staff is significant for us and paramount in having a good functionality of the hotel. At our hotel, we are all a family, which is why if you choose our hotel, we welcome you in our cozy and friendly home with our arms open. 

We want you to feel safe and comfortable, so all of our employees are professionals and trustworthy. Moreover, they are at your service at any given time if you need help.
